Provident Ecopolitan

At KIADB Aerospace Park, Bangalore

Provident Ecopolitan is one of the best residential projects located at Aerospace Park in Bengaluru. This project has been developed by well-known developer Provident Housing, an associate of Purvankara Limited, and promises an outstanding living experience with unmatched amenities. The development will have 2-bedroom, and 3-bedroom residential flats that are thoughtfully designed and come with world-class amenities

The project is spread over wide acres and will have 75% open space for the residents to enjoy. Some of the highlights of the project include a swimming pool, Tennis court, children's play area, clubhouse, gymnasium, jogging track, and landscaped gardens. There will also be a retail zone within the complex for everyday convenience.

About Provident

Provident Housing is a real estate development company based in Bangalore, India. The company was established in 1975 and has since then become one of the leading developers in the country, with a focus on affordable housing. Provident Housing is a subsidiary of Puravankara Limited, a well-known real estate brand in India.

Provident Housing has completed several residential projects in Bangalore, Chennai, Hyderabad, Coimbatore, Mangalore, and Goa. The company's projects range from affordable apartments to luxury villas, catering to a wide range of customers. Provident Housing is committed to delivering quality homes that are affordable and offer a comfortable living experience.

The company has a strong focus on sustainability and green initiatives, incorporating features like rainwater harvesting, solar power, and waste management systems in its projects. Provident Housing also offers a range of amenities like a clubhouse, swimming pool, gym, and sports facilities, among others.
